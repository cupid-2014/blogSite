### Eclipse引用本地Maven
--------

#### 修改eclipse引用maven配置
* 依次打开`Window --> Preferences --> Maven --> User Settings`，修改eclipse默认配置为本地maven
![Eclipse Maven配置](../../../image/maven/eclipse_001.png)
* 依次打开`Window --> Show View --> Other... --> Maven --> Maven Repositories`，重建索引

* *Eclipse每次更换目录都需要重新设置引用本地Maven*
